## Slide 1: The Problem We Solve

**Title:** Why Beans Inventory Management?

- Bean warehouse owners track inventory using paper notebooks or Excel spreadsheets
- Manual counting leads to errors — wrong stock levels, missed arrivals, lost sales
- No real-time visibility into what's in storage
- Staff struggle to generate reports for business decisions
- Myanmar bean businesses need a digital solution built for their workflow

> **Speaker Notes (20 sec):**
> Traditional bean warehouses in Myanmar rely on manual record-keeping. This leads to inventory discrepancies, missed orders, and wasted time. Our system digitizes the entire workflow — from arrivals to sales — giving owners real-time accuracy and control.

## Slide 2: Arrivals, Sales & Storage

**Title:** Track Every Bag In, Out, and Where It Lives

- **Arrivals:** Log incoming shipments with date, bean type, bags, and purchase price
- **Sales:** Record outgoing orders with customer info and sale price
- **Storage:** Organize beans by warehouse location, prevent over-selling
- Each transaction auto-updates stock levels in real-time
- Dashboard shows daily/weekly/monthly summaries with low-stock alerts

> **Speaker Notes (20 sec):**
> Every bean bag entering or leaving the warehouse gets recorded. The system tracks bean type, quantity, price, and date — and knows exactly where each bag is stored. Stock updates automatically. Owners see at a glance what's in stock, what's running low, and where it all is.

## Slide 4: Adjustments & Audit Trail

**Title:** Fix Mistakes with Full Accountability

- **Stock Adjustments:** Correct inventory when physical count differs from system
- Choose increase or decrease with reason (damage, recount, spoilage)
- Every adjustment is logged with timestamp and user
- **Audit Trail:** Complete history of who changed what and when
- Builds trust and accountability among warehouse staff

> **Speaker Notes (20 sec):**
> Sometimes physical counts don't match the system. Staff can make adjustments with a reason — damage, spoilage, or recount. Every change is logged with who did it and when. This audit trail ensures accountability and helps owners investigate discrepancies.
